The inpatient ward consists of 48 single rooms with individual en-suite bathrooms. Each room has a balcony with ranch slider access, Sky TV, free wi-fi and is furnished to a high standard.  The rooms are split into three wings, North, West and South.

Bharath Hospital has a small 3 bed High Dependency Room where patients who require dedicated nursing are cared for after surgery until stable.

Chefs in the kitchen provide an a la carte menu for patients unless there are dietary restrictions when a suitable menu will be offered. The meals are of an excellent standard and are approved by a registered dietician.

Nurses working at Grace have been chosen for their experience in surgical nursing, their passion for providing excellent patient care, and their drive for continuous improvement.

The ward functions supported by the Ward Manager, Duty Managers and two Nurse Practitioner Interns who ensure that patient care is consistent, caring and that patients can feel confident in the standard of care being delivered.
